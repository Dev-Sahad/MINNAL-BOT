# Privacy Policy for MINNAL BOT

**Effective Date:** April 30, 2026

### 1. Data Collection
MINNAL BOT does not collect or store personal user data such as names, emails, or phone numbers. We only process data required for the bot to function, which may include:
* **Server IDs:** To remember bot settings for your specific server.
* **Channel IDs:** To identify where to send media or logs.

### 2. Data Usage
The data collected is used solely to provide and improve the bot's features (media automation and utility). We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties.

### 3. Data Retention
User data is only kept as long as the bot is active in your server. If you remove the bot, your server configuration is automatically removed from our active database.

### 4. Contact
For any questions regarding privacy, you can contact the developer (Sahad) through the official support server: https://discord.gg/3cQcMpcjRA
